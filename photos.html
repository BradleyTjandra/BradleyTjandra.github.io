Bradley's photo competition

I don't have enough photos of myself or the events I organise. So I'm starting a bounty for people to help me have more photos.

I'll award $5 for photos of events I organise.

I'll award $10 for photos I put on my dating profile.

Extra information

The key criteria for a dating profile photo is that it:

  1. Makes me look dateable
  2. Reflects my personality
  3. Isn't too similar to other winners

The key criteria for event photos is that it's something I can look back on and have fond memories. E.g.

  1. People having a good time
  2. Something visually interesting
  3. Demonstrating the effort I put into the event

Prizes will be awarded whenever I decide. Photos will be selected based on my capricious whims, but you are more than welcome to advocate for why your photo is good. By submitting a photo you give me the right to use the photos for any and all of my nefarious plans.
