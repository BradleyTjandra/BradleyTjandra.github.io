How to find us

It's a bit confusing how to get to our apartment, so I've written up some instructions

  1. We live at Unit 107 at 222 Sussex Street Sydney
  2. When you arrive, you can buzz our apartment either before you enter the building (easiest) or at the panel to the right of the concierge desk
  3. Once we buzz you up, take the elevator to Level 29

If you get hopelessly lost you can also ask the concierge. Either say you're visiting Bradley and Rebecca, or Unit 107.
